Carbonara

Chef's Special: Carbonara

In this recipe we will cover the recipe for the original Italian Carbonara

Ingriedients

  • bacon
  • spagetti
  • parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs
  • salt + pepper

Instructions

  1. add bacon to a cold pan and cook to taste
  2. crack eggs in a bowl and add the parmesan cheese
  3. boil pasta water and add pasta
  4. when your pasta is cooked, add it to your bacon
  5. take your pan of the heat and add your cheese egg mixture
  6. add pepper and salt to taste
