Hamburger

Chef's special: Classic Hamburger

In this recipe we will demonstrate Wander's famous american hamburger.

Ingriedients

  • Hamburger Bun
  • Patty
  • Mayonaise
  • Cheddar
  • Onion
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato

Instructions

  1. Bake: Bake the patty and add salt + pepper to the meat
  2. Essemble:
    1. Put mayonaise on the bun
    2. Put the patty on the mayonaise
    3. Add cheddar + chopped onion
    4. Add the lettuce and tomato slice and top off with the top bun
  3. Enjoy!
